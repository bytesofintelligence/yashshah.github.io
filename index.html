About me

Hi, I'm Yash Shah, a Computing student at Imperial College London, where I was delighted to achieve a First Class in my first year. I'm passionate about technology and love building solutions that make a real difference, whether it's analysing complex datasets, optimising algorithms, or exploring new fields like machine learning in healthcare.

Throughout my journey, I have had the privilege of working on a range of projects, from leveraging GPS data to enhance athletic performance with the Rajasthan Royals to supporting charities during the pandemic with technical solutions. Recently, I've loved sharing experiences with others, through my podcast, "The Bytes of Intelligence Podcast", where I have been learning from inspiring guests. I'm also the Chair of the Imperial Jain Society, where I help build community and raise awareness on campus. Beyond tech, I'm a drone enthusiast, capturing unique travel moments and exploring my creativity through aerial photography.

Welcome to my portfolio website! Here, you'll find a collection of my projects, my thoughts on tech, and a glimpse into my journey so far. I am always excited to connect with others in any field, so feel free to reach out! Many thanks!
